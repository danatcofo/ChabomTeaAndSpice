About Chabom

Chabom Teas + Spices is a cozy shop located on Historic C-Street featuring fine culinary spices and loose leaf teas from around the world.

We carry a variety of tea pots and accessories to meet the tea drinkers needs. You can also find locally made baked goods, cards, necklaces, pottery, roasted coffee beans, candles and clothing.

You will find the largest selection of packaged and loose leaf teas, herbs and spices in Springfield, MO!

We provide a hot and iced tea service as well. You can try out a tea before you buy.

We have cozy little seating areas so you can enjoy your time in our shop or take a cup to go while exploring our wonderful Historic C-Street community!
